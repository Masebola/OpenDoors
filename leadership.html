Meet Our Pastors

Rev. Thabo Mkhize

Senior Pastor

Pastor Thabo has been leading Open Doors Foundation for over 15 years. With a heart for teaching and a passion for people, he is dedicated to helping others discover their purpose in Christ.

He holds a Master of Divinity and loves connecting with the community through outreach and fellowship.

Naledi Mkhize

Co-Pastor & Women's Ministry Leader

Naledi works alongside her husband, leading the Women's Ministry and overseeing community care. She is passionate about empowering women to walk in confidence and faith.

Her warm spirit and wise counsel make her a beloved mother figure to many in the congregation.
